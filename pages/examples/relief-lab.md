This interactive lab application created for the 
<a href="https://mountaincartography.icaci.org/activities/workshops/harrachov_czech/website/" target="_blank" rel="noepener">XIV ICA Mountain Cartography 
Workshop</a>, lets you experiment with various relief-shading
capabilities in cartolina-js in real time. Use the control panel to
adjust illumination direction, atmospheric parameters, and vertical
exaggeration, and observe the effect on the rendered terrain.
